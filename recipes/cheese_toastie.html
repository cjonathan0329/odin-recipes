Cheese Toastie

This is a delicious gooey grilled cheese sandwich!

Ingrdients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Cheese of choice
  • Butter or Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Spread butter or mayonnaise on to two slices of bread
  2. Put one slice of bread, spread side down onto a griddle
  3. Put as many slices of cheese as you want on that slice of bread
  4. Put the other slice of bread onto the fat stack of cheese
  5. Cook until golden brown on each side
  6. Enjoy!
