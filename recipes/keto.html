Keto Cauliflower Salad

This is a delicious keto friendly cauliflower salad!

Ingredients

  • Head of cauliflower
  • Head of lettuce
  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 1 small box of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 red tomatoe
  • 1 lb of bacon
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4th cup of parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. cook 1 lb of bacon
  2. chop bacon into crumbles
  3. chop head of cauliflower into bite size pieces
  4. chop head of lettuce into bite size pieces
  5. dice up the red onion
  6. mix mayonnaise and parmesan cheese
  7. mix altogether
